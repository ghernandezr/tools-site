Conversion Formulas

Length: Kilometers ↔ Miles

km → miles: miles = km × 0.621371
miles → km: km = miles × 1.60934

Examples:
100 km = 62.14 miles
26.2 miles (marathon) = 42.16 km
1 mile = 1.609 km

Weight: Kilograms ↔ Pounds

kg → lbs: lbs = kg × 2.20462
lbs → kg: kg = lbs × 0.453592

Examples:
70 kg = 154.32 lbs
150 lbs = 68.04 kg
1 kg = 2.205 lbs

Temperature: Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit

°C → °F: F = (C × 9/5) + 32
°F → °C: C = (F − 32) × 5/9

Key reference points:
0°C = 32°F (water freezes)
20°C = 68°F (room temperature)
37°C = 98.6°F (body temperature)
100°C = 212°F (water boils)

Frequently Asked Questions

How many miles is 1 kilometer?
1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles. As a quick rule of thumb, multiply km by 0.6 for a rough miles estimate. For more precision, use our converter above or the exact factor of 0.621371.
How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?
Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. Example: 25°C = (25 × 1.8) + 32 = 45 + 32 = 77°F. Quick estimate: double the Celsius temperature and add 30 (not precise but useful mentally). For example: 20°C → 20×2+30 = 70°F (actual: 68°F).
How many pounds is 1 kilogram?
1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds. Roughly 2.2 lbs per kg. To convert quickly in your head: multiply kg by 2, then add 10%. Example: 70 kg → 70 × 2 = 140, + 7 = 147 lbs (actual: 154.3 lbs — this approximation is less accurate for large values; use the calculator for precision).
What is 100°F in Celsius?
°C = (100 − 32) × 5/9 = 68 × 5/9 = 37.78°C. Note: 98.6°F (normal body temperature) = exactly 37°C. 100°C is the boiling point of water = 212°F.
How many km is a 5K race?
A 5K race is exactly 5 kilometers, which equals approximately 3.107 miles. A 10K is 10 km = 6.214 miles. A half marathon is 21.097 km = 13.109 miles. A full marathon is 42.195 km = 26.219 miles.
